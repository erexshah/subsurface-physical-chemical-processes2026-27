# Subsurface Physical-Chemical Processes

Welcome to the repository for the **Subsurface Physical-Chemical Processes** unit.

This repository provides course materials, assignments, and computational resources to support your learning throughout the unit. It includes lecture-related content, numerical simulation tutorials (including OPM-based workflows), and practical exercises designed to strengthen both theoretical understanding and applied modelling skills.

---

## 🌍 About the Unit

This course explores the fundamental mechanisms governing the behaviour of fluids, solutes, heat, and reactive species in subsurface environments. A central focus is the coupling between:

- **Physical transport processes**: flow, diffusion, dispersion, and heat transfer  
- **Chemical processes**: sorption, reactions, and phase behaviour  

Students will develop conceptual and mathematical models and apply numerical simulation tools to analyse real-world subsurface systems.

### Applications include:
- Geothermal energy systems  
- Subsurface energy storage  
- Carbon capture and storage (CCS)  
- Groundwater and environmental remediation

## 🎯 Learning Outcomes

By the end of this unit, you should be able to:

- Understand and describe coupled physical-chemical processes in porous media  
- Formulate governing equations for subsurface transport and reactions  
- Apply numerical simulation tools to solve complex subsurface problems  
- Interpret modelling results in the context of real-world applications
